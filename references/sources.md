# Paper Sources

Exact or near-canonical source links for the `dl-playground` paper set.

Notes:
- Prefer official proceedings / publisher / official archive.
- For older papers, DOI landing pages are often the safest stable canonical reference.
- For newer ML papers, `arXiv`, `PMLR`, `JMLR`, `OpenReview`, and conference proceedings pages are usually the best practical sources.
- A few entries still need a final manual verification pass.
